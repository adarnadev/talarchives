TALArchives.net is a mock website based on the fictional PC game, Terra Anima Legion of Anitos (TALA) Online. TALA Online is a role-playing game that forms a large part of the narrative thread in the Janus Silang series written by Edgar Calabia Samar.

Janus Silang at ang Tiyanak ng Tàbon is the first title in this series of novels for young adults and will be available by May 2014, published by Adarna House.
